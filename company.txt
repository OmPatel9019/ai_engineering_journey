ACME Tech Company - Employee Handbook

Leave Policy:
Employees get 20 days of paid leave per year.
Sick leave is 10 days per year.
Leave must be applied 2 weeks in advance except for emergencies.
Unused leave can be carried forward up to 5 days only.

Work Hours:
Office hours are 9 AM to 6 PM Monday to Friday.
Remote work is allowed on Wednesdays and Fridays.
Overtime is paid at 1.5x the hourly rate.

Salary and Benefits:
Salaries are paid on the 1st of every month.
Health insurance covers employee and immediate family.
Annual bonus is based on performance review in December.

Code of Conduct:
Employees must maintain confidentiality of company data.
Harassment of any kind will result in immediate termination.
All disputes must be reported to HR within 48 hours.
